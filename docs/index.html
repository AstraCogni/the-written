Quick Start
  1. Step 1 — Questions: Upload a TSV file, paste questions directly, or click load sample EMS questions to try it immediately with built-in examples.
  2. Step 2 — Logo: Upload your department seal or patch. Optional but recommended.
  3. Step 3 — Configure: Set your course title, department name, pass threshold, question count, and shuffle behavior.
  4. Step 4 — Colors: Choose a preset or dial in custom colors to match your organization.
  5. Click ▶ Preview Quiz to see exactly what your crew will see — no LMS needed.
  6. Click Generate SCORM Package — a ZIP file downloads automatically.
  7. Log into your LMS, upload the ZIP, and assign it to your learners.
Question Bank Format

Tab-separated values (.tsv), one question per row. First row is a header — always skipped.

QuestionType [TAB] QuestionText [TAB] Choice1 [TAB] Choice2... [TAB] CorrectIndex [TAB] Feedback
  • QuestionType: MC (multiple choice) or TF (true/false)
  • CorrectIndex: Zero-based — first choice = 0, second = 1, etc.
  • Feedback: Optional — explanation shown after the learner answers. Leave the column off entirely for no feedback.
  • Up to 5 answer choices. Empty columns between last choice and CorrectIndex are ignored.

Open sample_questions.tsv in Excel to see the format in action.

Logo / Seal
  • PNG with transparent background recommended
  • 500×500px minimum — displayed as a circle in the quiz header and start screen
  • Embedded directly in the package — no external files needed
Generated Package
  • Compatible with any SCORM 1.2 or SCORM 2004 LMS
  • Tested on NinthBrain, SCORM Cloud, Moodle, and Canvas
  • Records per-question interaction data for LMS item analysis
  • Learners can resume mid-quiz across browser sessions
